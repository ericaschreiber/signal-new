Agreements

  • Take 100% responsibility for your physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual well-being throughout the Forum.
  • Stay curious in the face of all challenging feedback, including looking at the opposite of all of your stories.
  • Commit to giving direct feedback to peers and facilitators.
  • Participate in the entire session, including arriving on time and staying until the end.
  • Maintain a 10-minute minimum daily meditation practice.
  • Meet weekly with learning partners for a 45-minute call.
  • Attend all retreats and small group coaching calls unless an exception has been negotiated.
  • Post weekly on the private forum discussion board and comment on others’ updates.
  • Pay in full by the due date. There are no refunds.
  • Complete homework assignments, Enneagram assessment/interview, and read The 15 Commitments of Conscious Leadership.
